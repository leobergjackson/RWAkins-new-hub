The issuer bond is a first-loss capital deposit in USDT. It demonstrates the issuer's commitment and protects coverage holders. The bond equals 5% of the token's market capitalization and is held in the IssuerBond smart contract. If a default occurs, the bond is liquidated first before pool funds are used. The bond is returned (minus a small protocol fee) upon successful wind-down.

Legal Entity Verification

Step 4 of 5
Get this from the Attestation Service after submitting your legal entity registration.
  1. Visit the Attestation Service portal
  2. Connect your wallet (the same one registering here)
  3. Navigate to "Create Attestation" and select the CoverFi Legal Entity schema
  4. Fill in your legal entity details: company name, jurisdiction, registration number
  5. Submit the attestation and pay the gas fee
  6. Copy the attestation UID from the transaction receipt
  7. Paste the UID in the field above

The attestation proves your legal entity exists and is linked to your wallet address on-chain.
