How CoverFi Works

Six steps from registration to payout. Fully on-chain, fully transparent.

1

Issuer Registers

RWA issuers post a 5% bond and get attested by 3 independent verifiers.

2

IRS Score Calculated

5-dimensional risk score across NAV, attestation, repayment, collateral, and activity.

3

Coverage Purchased

Token holders buy protection. Premium set algorithmically: 1600 × e(-0.001386 × IRS).

4

LPs Provide Liquidity

Senior (70%) and junior (30%) tranches earn yield from premiums. Junior absorbs first-loss.

5

Default Detected

2-of-3 trusted attestors confirm default. Oracle triggers payout engine automatically.

6

Payout Executed

USDT sent to verified ERC-3643 holders. SubrogationNFT minted for Foundation recovery.
