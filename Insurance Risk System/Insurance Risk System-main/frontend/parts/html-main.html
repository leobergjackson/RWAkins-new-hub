How CoverFi Works

1
Issuer Registers
RWA issuers post a 5 BNB bond and get attested by 3 independent verifiers (custodian, legal, auditor)
2
IRS Score Calculated
5-dimensional risk score (0-1000) across NAV, attestation, repayment, collateral and activity metrics
3
Coverage Purchased
Token holders buy protection. Premium set algorithmically: 1600 x e^(-0.001386 x IRS) bps/year
4
LPs Provide Liquidity
Senior (70%) and junior (30%) tranches earn yield from premiums. Junior absorbs first-loss risk
5
Default Detected
2-of-3 trusted attestors confirm default event. Oracle triggers payout engine automatically
6
Payout Executed
USDT sent to verified ERC-3643 holders. SubrogationNFT minted for Foundation recovery rights
