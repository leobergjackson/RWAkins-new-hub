Recovery Process

1

SubrogationNFT Minted

When a default is processed by the PayoutEngine, a SubrogationNFT is automatically minted. It records the issuer token, default type, liquidation amounts, and payout block as immutable on-chain evidence.

2

Evidence Container

The NFT serves as a portable, transferable evidence container. It captures the bond, junior tranche, and senior tranche amounts liquidated, along with the number of insured holders compensated.

3

Foundation Initiates Recovery

The CoverFi Foundation, as the NFT holder, initiates legal recovery proceedings against the defaulted issuer. The SubrogationNFT provides cryptographic proof of the default event and amounts owed.

4

Timeline & Resolution

Legal recovery timelines vary by jurisdiction and default type. The Foundation pursues recovery through appropriate legal channels. Recovered funds are returned to the protocol treasury for redistribution.
